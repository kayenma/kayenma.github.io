How it Works!

This app allows users to quickly and easily find someone nearby who is interested in doing the same activity within 6 hours. Here's a step-by-step explanation of how the app works:

  1. Upon opening the app, the user is prompted to enable their GPS location to identify where they are located.
  2. The app then gives the user a list of options to choose from such as drinking, bowling, or going to the cinema.
  3. Once the user selects the activity they want to do, the app prompts them to take a selfie to capture the moment.
  4. The user can also write a few words about the activity, but it's not required.
  5. The timer then starts for 6 hours, during which the selfie and activity information are available to other users nearby.
  6. After 6 hours, the selfie and activity information are deleted from the app.
  7. During the 6 hours, the user can wait to receive messages from people nearby to chat and meet.
  8. The user can also see who is nearby and what they like to do within the 6-hour timeframe.
